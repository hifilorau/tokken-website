Introducing Tokken
A Better Way to Buy Marijuana

Check out the Kickstarter

There is a huge problem in the legalized marijuana industry. It's a cash based economy in a world where cash is quickly becoming obsolete. Tokken changes that.

For consumers, Tokken is a better way to buy marijuana or goods without having to use cash or incur ATM fees.

For dispensaries, Tokken offers an easy, straight forward, and legal path to regulated US banking.
