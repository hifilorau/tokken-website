Liberate Your Wallet

Tokken is a consumer-focused, secure payment system that replaces cash. Our platform leverages latest technological innovation in cryptography and data management to offer banking and payment services to a growing segment of the under-banked and un- banked.

How it works

Tokken is both a consumer mobile wallet and a corresponding merchant add-on that integrates into multiple, widely used point-of-sale (POS) systems.

Each Tokken transaction is protected with our bank-grade security.

Instant funding

When customers pay with Tokken our merchants are funded instantly.

Where does the money go

While your money never leaves the banking system, we issue digital tokens to merchants at the time the transaction.

What to do with tokkens?

Once merchants get Tokkens, they have options.

  • 1. Liquidate and use the proceeds to pay bills, payroll, taxes, transfer to another account, or
  • 2. Pay everyone with Tokkens.
